Steak and boiled vegetables

Description

This is a good, tasty and healthy recipe that is quick and simple to cook.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Oil or tallow
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetables of choice

Steps

  1. Wash and chop up vegetables
  2. Bring a saucepan of water to the boil
  3. Whilst the water is heating up, heat up the frying pan with oil in it
  4. Add the vegetables to the boiling water, and boil for 4-6 minutes
  5. Season the steak, and rub it in with oil before adding it to the frying pan. Fry for 5-7 minutes for medium rare, depending on the thickness of the steak.
